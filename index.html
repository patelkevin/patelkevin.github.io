ABOUT ME
HOW I WORK
MORE WORK
I love to create intuitive, beautiful, user friendly and simple interfaces. I believe good design is usable, simple, and attractive, and is ultimately the result of a designer with a comprehensive understanding of the user and business needs

I have 5+ years of experience designing digital products. My expertise covers the entire digital product design process for both websites and mobile apps, including visual identity, user experience design and user interface design.
01. Research
I conduct immersive research to align objectives, define the target and validate an idea before developing any solution.
04. Testing
Once the project is ready I conduct usability tests to validate the solution with real users before deploying the product.
02. Exploration
Once we empathize with the business goals and target needs I build visual concepts that bring us clarity and awareness of the right solution.
05. Iteration
Sometimes your project  will demand adjustments and rethinking based on users criteria. I apply improvements on the project before launching.
03. Design
After understanding all the aspects of the product and business I sketch, wireframe and design a beautiful and scalable interface.
06. Deploy
Once the project is validated we are ready to develop, launch and publish the product.
