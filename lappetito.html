OVERVIEW

L’Appetito is a fusion restaurant, that offers fusion, home-made artisanal italian dishes along with classical chinese cusinie. Alongside they have a variety of menu box options which consist of a combo of main course, desert and drinks.

Currently they do not have an online presence, I was tasked with creating the landing page for the restaurant and also the online order pages. Our goal was to design the complete website and make it user friendly and responsive.
